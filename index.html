DAILY WELLNESS

Why blood sugar wellness matters

Supporting overall wellness begins with practical daily choices. A balanced eating pattern, regular movement, sufficient sleep and professional medical guidance can all play an important role.

Some adults also choose dietary supplements as part of their wellness routine. Supplements are not a substitute for medical care, prescribed treatment or healthy lifestyle habits.
